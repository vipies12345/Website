About MatView

Who We Are

MatView Private Limited is a cutting-edge technology company specializing in renewable energy simulation tools and microgrid components. We offer Wind Emulator-based microgrids, Solar Array Simulators, Grid Simulators, and a wide range of programmable DC loads.

Our Vision

To innovate and provide efficient, reliable, and affordable renewable energy test solutions that empower researchers, academic institutions, and industries to simulate, test, and validate their systems with ease and precision.

What We Offer

Our product lineup includes high-quality Grid-Tied Inverters, Wind and Solar Emulators, Programmable Loads, Power Analyzers, and Data Acquisition Systems tailored for energy labs, educational institutions, and industrial R&D.
